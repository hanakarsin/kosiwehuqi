Young Man Wows Many with Acrobatic Moves, Turns Himself to a 'Car' and Rolls Like Tyre

  • In a video, a young man displayed acrobatic skills that got many people on social media thrilled
  • The man entertained onlookers with different styles including somersaulting and standing on his hands and also putting both legs on his head
  • He also maintained the position of a car, but he didn't only maintain this position, he rolled himself across the road like a tyre

A young man has wowed social media users with his acrobatic moves in an impressive video that was shared online.

In the video posted on Facebook by Ayo Ojeniyi, the man started off by displaying martial art moves before somersaulting and standing on his hands and sending his legs backwards with his head between them.

He could be seen doing push-ups while maintaining this position.

The young man thrilled onlookers with different styles. While standing on his hands, his legs rested on his head comfortably and he twisted his waist as if dancing to music, which got people laughing.

Interesting, the highlight of the video was when he turned himself into a 'car' and rolled himself across the road like a tyre.

He maintained a position that had his legs jutting out from his armpits, closed and released his fingers multiple times as if indicating that a car's lights were switched on.

He then switched his imaginary gear and drove, before rolling himself across the road.
