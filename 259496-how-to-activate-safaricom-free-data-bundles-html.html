Safaricom free data bundles 2023: how to activate in Kenya

Safaricom's priority is the happiness of its subscribers. In addition to providing the best customer experience, they also focus on providing their customers with the best value for their money. To that end, they periodically develop offers for free data bundles to motivate their pool of customers. Here is a simple guide on how to get Safaricom free data bundles.

While transitioning from Safaricom data offers in 2022, the company has something for you in 2023. Learn how you can activate their 2023 free bundles.

Safaricom free data bundles in 2023

How do I get free Safaricom data bundles? Here are some tips on how to get free data from Safaricom.

Using Zuri referral plan

You can buy minutes, check your balance, and manage your subscriptions through Zuri. To activate free Zuri bundles, you need to refer any Safaricom subscriber to use Zuri services. To use Zuri on WhatsApp, first save Safaricom's customer service phone number, 0722000100, in your phone's contact list.

    To promote their app, Safaricom awards you 500MB free for downloading their app. To activate the free bundles, follow the steps below.

  • Register yourself on the app using your Safaricom line.

    • Using Baze bonus

    Baze offers a video entertainment service where you can access a vast selection of short videos in music, news, and sports. To activate the free bundles:

  • Go to your browser, key in www.baze.co.ke, and then register using your name and Safaricom line.
  • To watch content, you must subscribe at Ksh10 for daily content. Alternatively, you can subscribe for a "Daily Combo" of "Content Plus Data" at Ksh20 with 200MB.
  • You can also dial *544#, then go to video bundles on option 6. Then, choose "Unlock Baze Bonus" on option 0. You should have a Ksh10 credit to be awarded a Baze pass and access 500MB free daily.
  • You will receive the free bundles for the next three days, after which you can continue subscribing or opt-out.

    Subscribing to an all-in-one monthly plan

    You can switch to Safaricom monthly plans and access free internet services such as streaming and browsing. To activate this service:

  • Dial *544# and go to the "Monthly Bundles NEW" option 4.
  • Choose option 3 of "All In One".
  • Select the type of bundles you want, such as 5GB +2GB free YouTube + 400 minutes +1000 SMS for only Ksh1050.
  • You will also be able to access free unlimited WhatsApp access for one month.

    • Buying a new phone from any Safaricom store

    Another efficient way to activate free bundles is to buy a new phone from a Safaricom store in the country.

    Different phones have different amounts of packs that can be reclaimed after purchase; a low-end phone can give you 100MB, while a high-end phone can give you up to 8GB of free data bundles.
