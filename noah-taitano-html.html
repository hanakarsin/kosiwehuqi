Noah Boat - Age, Family, Bio

A director and editor for the YouTube channel loveliveserve who co-creates a variety of entertaining material alongside his partner Ryan Burton. They feature musical.ly compilation reaction videos, comedy skits, and more for their 7 million subscribers. He was featured in the "If Lil Nas X was in your class" video, which received over 22 million views since it was released in August of 2019. 

Before Fame

As a child, he would make webcam videos on his Dell laptop. He upgraded to a camcorder in 2010. He and Ryan created their channel in November of 2010, but did not publish a first video until one year later. The video was titled "BEST DAY 3VER."

Trivia

He graduated from North Point High School in 2015. He would attend Drexel University as a film student. He has also created the Hour43 clothing company which has designed and sold leisure wear. 

Family Life

He was born in Washington D.C. He has an older sister named Anna and a younger sister named Amy.

Associated With

He and Ryan have reacted to content produced by web stars such as Bryan Le, Baby Ariel, Loren Beech, and more.
