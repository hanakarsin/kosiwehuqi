Asmxlls - Age, Family, Bio

Popular social media star who posts lip-sync and comedy videos to his asmxlls TikTok account. His videos have earned him over 400,000 followers on the app. 

Before Fame

He launched his TikTok account in January 2020. 

Trivia

He started his Asmxlls YouTube channel in July 2019 and has accumulated over 130,000 subscribers on the platform. The channel features him rapping in a video titled "The Start Freestyle (Official Music Video)". 

Family Life

On August 27, 2020, he posted a Q&A video with his sister to his YouTube channel. In February 2021, his mom appeared on his TikTok account. 

Associated With

He posted a TikTok video set to the Young Thug song "Special" in May 2020.
