Flavour Has Slept With Chidinma, They Are Not Just Friends

Controversial rapper, Darlington Okoye, also known as Speed Darlington, has alleged that his colleagues Flavour N’abania and Chidinma Ekile have slept together.

The media personality made the claim in Igbo language during a live session on Instagram.

He opined that the body language between the duo suggests that Flavour has definitely had carnal knowledge of Chindinma.

According to him, the way Flavour holds Chidinma suggests that something has taken place under the sheet.

Darlington insisted that he is not a child that could be made to believe that the musicians simply share a close relationship with no undercurrent.

He went on to disclose that he hasn’t slept with any celebrity and he’s really longing for it.

In a translated version, he said: “I have not slept with any celebrity in my life. Flavour has slept with Chidinma, yes, you can’t hold someone’s woman that way if you and her haven’t done something, f**k outta here, I’m not a child yes, you can’t hold someone’s woman like that if it’s a platonic relationship.

“So no nigga, I don’t give a f**k. Let me sleep with celebrities also, I have not slept with a celebrity, do you understand?”
