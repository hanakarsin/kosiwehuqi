Victor Arroyo - Age, Family, Bio

House guest from the 18th season of CBS' Big Brother. He was notable for being the first Big Brother competitor to return to the house after being evicted twice in a single season.

Before Fame

Before joining the Big Brother house, he was occupied as an operations manager at an LA Fitness gym.

Trivia

His Instagram account elfitvic is filled with workout videos and is followed by 260,000 people.

Family Life

He has an older sister named Ashly Arroyo. He began dating fellow Big Brother contestant Nicole Franzel in September 2017 and the two later got married. On July 23, 2021, they welcomed their first child together, a son named Arrow.

Associated With

He formed an alliance with Paul Abrahamian on Big Brother.
