Alondra Delgado - Age, Family, Bio

Television and film actress who is widely recognized for her work as Vanessa Montes on the hit series All American. She has also appeared on three episodes of Mayans M.C.

Before Fame

In 2006, she was cast in the made for television movie Angels Perdidos.

Trivia

She guest starred on a 2019 episode of Vida.

Family Life

She was born in Los Angeles, CA. 

Associated With

She was cast alongside Daniel Ezra and Samantha Logan on All American.
