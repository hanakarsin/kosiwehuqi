Tinubu, Osinbajo, Osoba No Longer See Restructuring As Priority

published on July 31, 2024
The leader of social-political group Afenifere, Pa Ayo Adebanjo, has berated the national leader of the All Progressives Congress, APC, Bola Tinubu, over his stand on restructuring. In an interview with PUNCH on Friday, the 93-year-old statesman accused the former Lagos State governor of no longer seeing restructuring as a priority. He also lamented that some APC leaders including Vice-President Yemi Osinbajo; Tinubu, Bisi Akande, Olusegun Osoba, amongst others, promised restructuring during the 2015 electioneering but reneged on their promises after President Muhammadu Buhari won the election.
