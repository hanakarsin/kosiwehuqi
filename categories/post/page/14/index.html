Super Eagles Player Henry Onyekuru Signs For Greece's Olympiakos

published on August 13, 2024
24-year-old Nigerian AS Monaco striker Henry Onyekuru has signed with defending Greek champions Olympiakos Piraeus. Recruited in 2019 by AS Monaco from English club Everton for 17 million euros, the Nigerian international has only played 8 official matches with the Monegasques. He had been loaned two consecutive seasons at Galatasaray, Turkey. According to various sources familiar with the matter, the amount of the transfer would be five million euros with possible bonuses and an incentive on a potential capital gain in the event of resale. FG Reveals Cause Of Train Derailment In Niger State

published on August 12, 2024
The Nigerian Railway Corporation (NRC) has reacted to the derailment of a Lagos-Kano passenger train in Niger State on Saturday. Naija News reports that the train derailed from its track at Bode Sadu-Jebba Section within the Jebba area in Niger State. It was learned that the luggage vans at the rear were involved in the mishap and other parts of the train conveying passengers continued the journey.
