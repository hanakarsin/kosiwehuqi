The Nigeria Police Force (NPF) has warned members of the public against falling prey to a fake recruitment website impersonating the authentic police recruitment site. The Force Public Relations Officer, Olumuyiwa Adejobi who gave the warning in a statement on Wednesday said a fake portal not associated with the force is currently soliciting applications from members of the public. Senate Confirms Buratai, Olonisakin, Others As Non-career Ambassadors

published on June 16, 2024
The Nigerian Senate has confirmed the immediate past service chiefs as non-career ambassadors. Naija News reports that those confirmed are former Chief of Army Staff, Tukur Buratai; former Chief of Defence Staff, Gabriel Olonisakin; former Chief of Air Staff, Ibok Ibas; and former Chief of Naval Staff, Abubakar Sadique. Recall that  Buhari had recommended the appointment of all immediate past service chiefs as non-career ambassadors.
