Chef Dammy Speaks On Receiving N2 Million, Car Gift After 120-Hour Cook-A-Thon

Naija News reports that the Ekiti-based chef made the clarification in a video shared online, describing the talks online as false. According to Chef Dammy, the money gifts she received during and after the cooking exercise didn’t make up to N2 million as widely speculated.
