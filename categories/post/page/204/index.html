Again, Naira Regains Value Against US Dollar

published on April 14, 2024
Nigeria’s currency, the Naira, has again regained some value against the US dollar at the parallel market. Naija News understands that the dollar slipped against Naira on Friday, with the local currency exchanging below N1000 in the parallel market. The Naira has been regaining its ground against the widely patronized US currency in the last few weeks, a development considered remarkable since it exchanged above N1000. From ₦1,150 in the parallel market on Thursday, the dollar exchanged for N950 on Friday evening and below in the morning, gaining over N200.
