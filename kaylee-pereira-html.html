Kaylee Pereira - Age, Family, Bio

Content creator who shot to fame after starting her @kayleepereiraa TikTok account. Her impressive dancing and lip-syncing skills have helped her pick up over 800,000 fans and more than 23 million Likes.

Before Fame

In July 2017, she uploaded her premiere YouTube video titled MY GO-TO MAKE UP LOOK FOR SPECIAL EVENTS!! W/ INEXPENSIVE PRODUCTS!

Trivia

Her most-watched YouTube recording of 2019 was "Zaful Try-on Haul!!!" She is a member of the content creator collective The Vault.

Family Life

She is of Portuguese and Thai descent.

Associated With

She released a 2020 TikTok duet that was set to the Freddie Dredd track "Opaul."
