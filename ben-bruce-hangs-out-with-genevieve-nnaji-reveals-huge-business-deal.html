Ben Bruce Hangs Out With Genevieve Nnaji, Reveals Huge Business Deal

Nigerian business mogul and former Senator, Ben Murray-Bruce has revealed a huge business plan with veteran Nollywod actress, Genevieve Nnaji.

Naija News earlier reported that Genevieve reappeared at a public function, stirring reactions months after reports made the rounds online that she was suffering from mental illness.

The veteran actress also stayed away from social media, raising concerns amongst her fans.

Following her sudden disappearance, an Instagram blogger alleged that Genevieve Nnaji was hospitalized In America over mental illness.

The reports further noted that the 43-year-old actress had been on drugs for a long time, but was covered up by friends and family.

However, in viral photos that surfaced on social media, Genevieve Nnaji was spotted publicly delivering a speech at an event in Abidjan.

The Nigerian actor, director, and producer who looks chubbier and healthier spoke to delegates at the 2022 edition of Creative Africa Nexus, discussing the importance of the film industry to development in Africa.

Ben Bruce Meets Genevieve

Ben Murray-bruce, who also attended the event in Abidjan took to the microblogging platform, Twitter to share a photo of the moment he met with Nnaji.

According to the Nigerian politician, he is working on a movie deal with Genevieve, that would increase her film box office numbers.

He tweeted: “Working on a deal with @GenevieveNnaji1 so that all her movies will be produced at the BMB studios and film academy at Eko Atlantic City.”
