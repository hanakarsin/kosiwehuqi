When you're single, the thing you want most in the world is a great relationship. When you're in a great relationship, the thing you want most in the world is to mess it up and be off the hook.

OK so that's a generalization, but how else can you explain why people go to such crazy lengths to create drama in their relationships when they could otherwise be great? Or, and this is a lot more common: why is it that people rush to get into relationships that they knew from the beginning would be a nightmare to deal with?

I don't know, but if it's any consolation, humans have been dealing with relationship issues since the dawn of time. From Renoir figures fighting to Dutch master paintings depicting the most ancient of spats, men and women have always been trying to cope with their deepest emotional problems. 

I mean, why else in the world would these captions on classical art historical paintings fit so well, otherwise? Exactly. There's no other reason.

These memes are all so accurate, I don't even know which one I'd be if I had to go through the painstaking process of having to tag myself. Maybe I have better perspective on my friend group, because I know without a doubt who I want to DM each of these memes to. 

In fact, the concept of the page itself is so inspired, I want to scroll this list all over again just to laugh at the memes even harder. 

*Sigh* love is timeless, and so is all the stuff that goes along with it.
