Prophet Predicts What May Happen To Buhari, Osinbajo Before The End Of 2021

President Muhammadu Buhari has been urged not to get involved in corrupt conducts or bloodshed this period of his second tenure.

In a series of prophecies delivered to newsmen on Saturday, Apostle Paul Okikijesu of the Christ Apostolic Miracle Ministry noted that Vice President Yemi Osinbajo might take over leadership from the president before the end of their second term in office.

Okikijesu admonished Nigerians to earnestly pray for Buhari, noting that the case of late President Musa Ya’Radua and Goodluck Jonathan in May, 2010 may likely repeat itself and hence Osinbajo will take over.

The prophet insists that Buhari should not pay attention to wrong advice from people in his cabinet and those who surrounded him, so that he may live long.

“Thus saith the Lord: People should pray seriously for him [Buhari], so that when the looming crisis bursts, it will not be out of his control and harm this man,” the prophet said.

He added: “People should still pray for him because he is their President so that the crown will not be taken away from him and given to his neighbor who is better than him.

“So that the Vice President will not take over his position just like what happened during the time of Ya’radua and Jonathan.

“He should not get involved in corrupt conducts or bloodshed during this period, says the Lord God of hosts.

“Thus says the Lord: He should not listen to bad and inappropriate advice of the people who surrounded him; who are muddling up his government. So that his journey will not be ruined.”

Meanwhile, the leader of INRI Evangelical spiritual church, Primate Elijah Ayodele, warned the United States President, Joe Biden to pray about his health.

Ayodele said he foresees the President-elect having serious issues with his health, a condition that will bring uncertainty to his second term ambition.
