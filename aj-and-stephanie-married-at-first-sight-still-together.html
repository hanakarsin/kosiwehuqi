"I'm the last one in my family to be married and I'm just looking for that lifetime partnership," she continued. "Being together with that person no matter what, through thick and thin. I'm ready to not be alone in my life anymore."

Staffing company owner AJ Vollemoeller, 37, also couldn't believe he was headed to his own wedding day. "It hit me, I'm finally going to have a wife. And I cannot wait to meet her and hopefully impress her," he told the cameras while trying on tuxedos.
