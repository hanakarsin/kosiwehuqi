Ronald Jenkees - Age, Family, Bio

Keyboardist who is best known for having risen to prominence via his eponymous YouTube channel. He has released various albums as a result of his YouTube popularity. He released his first self-titled album of synthesized instrumental songs in 2007 independently.

Before Fame

He began his YouTube channel on April 19, 2006. He posted his first video, "My Roommate needs to do the dishes!", there on April 21, 2006.

Trivia

Since its creation, his YouTube channel has amassed more than 400,000 subscribers. On November 6, 2014, he released his 4th independent album Alpha Numeric.

Family Life

He was born and raised within the United States.

Associated With

He composed the theme song for Bill Simmons' podcast.
