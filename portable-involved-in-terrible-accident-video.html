Portable Involved In Terrible Accident (Video)

Fast-rising musician and composer, Portable Omolalomi born Habeeb Okikiola has escaped death after he was involved in a terrible accident.

Naija News reports that the incident occurred on Saturday, May 28th, 2022.

In the video which the controversial singer shared on the Instastory captured the moment, he was expressing his gratitude to God for escaping what he claimed to be a trap by the evildoers, whilst showing off the damaged vehicle.

According to him, they may be plotting against him because he is now a superstar.

Thankfully, the singer left the scene of the incident unscathed and healthy.

Meanwhile, Portable has responded to the Headies statement condemning his death threat to his fellow nominees if he losses the award.

Responding to the statement, Portable in a video shared on his page earlier this morning apologised over the death threat saying he wasn’t going to fight anyone but was merely joking about it.

Portable admitted he had done wrong saying he’s sorry for the usage of such words.

He, however, insisted that the organisers of Headies should give him space and an award because he’s currently taking over the streets.
