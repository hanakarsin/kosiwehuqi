Saraki Invites 7-yr-old Comedienne, Emmanuella, To Senate

Commedienne Emmanuella Samuel

Senate President, Bukola Saraki, has invited the 7-year-old Nigerian comedienne, Emmanuella Samuel to the upper legislative chamber.

Saraki, who described her story as an inspiration, made the invitation on his official Twitter and Facebook accounts on Sunday.

His invitation followed an announcement Miss Samuel made on her official Instagram page that she was set to feature in a Disney Hollywood film.

Miss Samuel had written, “Thanks @disneystudios God bless everyone whose support has added to bringing us here. I never dreamed of being here so soon. I miss Success. I love you all.”

He asked the commedienne to come to the Senate to discuss how to develop young talents in the Nigerian creative industry.

“Congratulations Emmanuella! Make sure you make them laugh over there.

“Your story is an inspiration. Come by the Nigerian Senate sometime, let’s discuss how we can develop the potential of our young talents in the creative industry,” Saraki said.
