Despite Claims Of Living In Same House, May Ignores Yul Edochie's Birthday Message

May Edochie, the wife of Nollywood actor Yul, has ignored her husband’s surprise birthday message as she expresses appreciation to his aunt, Rita Edochie.

Naija News reports that this is coming amidst Yul’s claim that they still living together in their home in Lagos State.

Recall that the 40-year-old movie star took to his Instagram page on Friday to share May’s photos accompanied by a lovely birthday message to her.

He wrote: “Happy birthday to my wife, my CEO extraordinaire QUEEN MAY YUL-EDOCHIE. I wish you many more beautiful and successful years. May God continue to guide you, bless you and protect you and bless you beyond imagination. Love you forever.”

However, a check on Yul’s birthday post on Saturday revealed that May failed to acknowledge the birthday post but responded to her husband’s aunt, Rita Edochie.

Yul Edochie Speaks On Strained Relationship May

Yul Edochie, recently made a new revelation about his marriage, amidst rumours that his first wife, May has left their home.

The actor while tackling a blogger who referred to May as his ‘estranged wife’ revealed that they are still living together in their home, in Lagos.

While schooling the blogger on the meaning of estranged, the actor said they still leave together in the same house and people can go and verify it.

He wrote: “You know I have a lot of respect for you. This your caption no gel naa. An estranged wife means a wife who is no longer living with the husband. Both of us are still living in the same house, in our mansion in Ajah. So how is she estranged? Peter Obi will always say ‘go and verify. You didn’t verify this one before writing.”
