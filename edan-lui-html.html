Edan Lui - Age, Family, Bio

Singer and actor best known for his role in the band Mirror. He has released his own pop music and has released multiple singles under the name Edan. His film debut is set for 2022.

Before Fame

He attended Salesian Yip Hon Millennium Primary School in Kwai Chung and Ho Fung College, achieving high grades. 

Trivia

He can play the piano as well as sing. He made his acting debut in 2019 on the TV show Showman Show.

Family Life

His sister Lui Wai Yu often appears on his Instagram.

Associated With

One of his fellow band members is pop singer Keung To.
