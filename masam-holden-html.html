Masam Holden - Age, Family, Bio

Television and film actor who is recognized for his portrayal of Michael Sullivan on the 2012 mini-series Halo 4: Forward Unto Dawn. He was also cast in the 2013 feature film The Spectacular Now.

Before Fame

He made his professional acting debut on a 2005 episode of Without a Trace.

Trivia

In 2007, he lent his voice to an episode of the animated series American Dad!

Family Life

His brother Alec Holden is also an actor.

Associated With

He was cast in The Spectacular Now which starred Miles Teller and Shailene Woodley.
