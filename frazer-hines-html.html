Frazer Hines - Age, Family, Bio

Portrayed the role of Joe Sugden on British soap "Emmerdale" from 1972 to 1994.

Before Fame

As a young boy, he attended Corona Theatre School.

Trivia

Prior to his role on "Emmerdale," he appeared on the British drama series "Doctor Who" as Jamie McCrimmon.

Family Life

He married second wife Liz Hobbs in 1994, but divorced in 2003.

Associated With

He once dated Liza Goddard.
