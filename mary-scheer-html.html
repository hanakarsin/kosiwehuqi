Mary Scheer - Age, Family, Bio

Comedic actress known as an original Mad TV cast member. She also played Freddie's mother in the Nickelodeon series iCarly and Gladys on Disney's Bunk'd. 

Before Fame

She had her first small acting role in 1989 on L.A. on $5 a Day. 

Trivia

She appeared on such Nickelodeon series as iCarly and The Penguins of Madagascar.

Family Life

She was born in Michigan. She later raised two children.

Associated With

Her many Mad TV impressions included an impersonation of actress Susan Sarandon.
