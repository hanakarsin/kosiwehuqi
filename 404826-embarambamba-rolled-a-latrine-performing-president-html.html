Embarambamba once rolled into a latrine while performing for the president

- Chris Embarambamba remembers late President Daniel Arap Moi was among the first people to notice his talent

- So, he often had the privilege of entertaining the late ex-president

- One day, while Embarambamba was performing his dance, he rolled into a nearby pit latrine that had been covered using leaves and shrubs

The gospel singer and energetic performer sat down with TUKO.co.ke’s Kevin Phillips Momanyi and gave an extensive peek into the beginning of his dancing journey.

The daring man who throws himself from dangerous heights remembered rolling into a pit latrine back when he was young.

His love for getting down to the beat started when he was still young back in 1997 but it brought him a tinge of embarrassment from time to time.

Once while entertaining the president and his deputy, Embarambamba who was still a kid, curled himself into a tiny ball and started rolling.

Little did he know that nearby, there was a pit latrine that had been lightly covered by leaves and shrubs.

He continued his antics and ended up dropping into the pit.

“I remember two police officers were standing next to the latrine and one of them saw me roll into the hole. It was funny. He told his partner what he saw and the other officer could not believe it at first,” he reminisced.

According to the musician, the police officer extended the nozzle of his gun into the hole and asked Embarambamba to grab it.

He then pulled the performer out of the latrine.

Embarambamba had once stated that he is not a mad man.

According to him, his gift comes from God.

The Kisii celebrity therefore cautioned people against mistaking his talent for a disorder.
