Koala Puffs - Age, Family, Bio

Instagram personality who has won more than 700,000 followers with her marijuana-themed account, koala.puffs. On her account, she showcases various cannabis products and posts comedy videos aimed at smokers. She is also apart of a podcast called "puff.n.chat.podcast" on Instagram.

Before Fame

She has lived in Los Angeles, California. 

Trivia

She has a large presence on the marijuana-based social platform The WeedTube. 

Family Life

She was born in Ukraine and she moved to the United States in 2005. She is an only child. Her first name is Anjela.

Associated With

She has been featured on The Tipsy Bartender, the YouTube channel created by Skyy John.
