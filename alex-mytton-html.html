Alex Mytton - Age, Family, Bio

British reality star seen on the show Made in Chelsea who is also a touring DJ and producer. He was first introduced on Made in Chelsea in series 5 of the show.

Before Fame

He was educated at Lord Wandsworth College and Oxford Brookes.

Trivia

He was featured in the spin-off Made in Chelsea: NYC and returned to the show the following season for series 8 back in England.

Family Life

He is from London, England. He dated Nicola Hughes. He later began a relationship with model Georgina Howard. In 2019, his mom tragically passed away after succumbing to a battle with brain cancer. He has a younger sister named Izzy.

Associated With

He was once friends with Jessica Woodley, but the friendship ended when he began dating Nicola.
