Wizkid's Made in Lagos album breaks US iTunes record

- Nigerian artiste, Wizkid, set a new record on US iTunes with his recently released Made in Lagos album

- The superstar’s Made in Lagos album has become the highest-charting at number 3

- Wizkid is the first Nigerian artiste with an album peaking at number 3 in the US iTunes history

Nigerian superstar Ayo Balogun, professionally known as Wizkid is the man of the moment as his recently released Made in Lagos album broke a record on US iTunes.

In the post shared by American OAP, Mike Adam via his official handle on Twitter, he stated that Made in Lagos album has become the highest-charting album by a Nigerian artist in US iTunes history, peaking at number 3.

He wrote:

"#MadeInLagos by @wizkidayo has become the highest charting album by a Nigerian artist in US iTunes HISTORY (#3 peak)!!! This is beyond huge! Congrats #WizKid!"
