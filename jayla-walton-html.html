Jayla Walton - Age, Family, Bio

Celebrity family member and actress who is recognized for being the sister of Euphoria actor Javon Walton. As an actress herself, she appeared on the Amazon Prime series Utopia. In 2022, she appeared in the movie 1-800-Hot-Nite as the character Kelly.

Before Fame

She played volleyball throughout her youth and practiced various styles of dance.

Trivia

She was featured in a commercial advertisement for Nike. She also appeared in the documentary No Days Off.

Family Life

She is the daughter of DJ Walton and Jessica Walton. She has siblings named Javon Walton, Jaden Walton, and Daelo Walton.

Associated With

She was cast alongside John Cusack on Utopia.
