Prince Akintunde Congratulates Alhaji Aminu Suleiman (DG TSO), Others Over Asiwaju Tinubu's Victory

Chairman, Tinubu Support Organisation (TSO) Diaspora Coordinators forum, Prince Bidemi Akintunde, has congratulated Alhaji Aminu Suleiman (DG TSO), Mrs Ayodele Fatusin (TSO South-West Coordinator) and Hon. Tayo Shodeinde (Director, TSO Diaspora) over the victory of Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu at the All Progressives Congress (APC) presidential primary.

Naija News reports that Asiwaju Tinubu, a former Lagos State Governor, had defeated a former Transportation Minister, Rotimi Amaechi; Vice President Yemi Osinbajo; Senate President Ahmad Lawan and many others to clinch the presidential ticket of the APC.

Prince Bidemi Akintunde

Reacting to Tinubu’s victory, Prince Bidemi Akintunde, who is the Chairman, TSO Diaspora, Europe Coordinator, congrulated the TSO DG, executives and members all over the world.

He wrote: “Congratulations to Alhaji Aminu Suleiman (DG, Tinubu Support Organization)

“I’m so thrilled to see that you reached your goal, with your support for Asiwaju Bola Tinubu. You inspire so many people by having such ambitious dreams, then putting in the hard work to achieve them.

“Congratulations to you, the executives and the entire members of Tinubu Support Organization worldwide.

“I’m incredibly proud of you sir as you have led the group with total determination and dedication towards ensuring that we mobilise for our principal Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu.”

According to Akintunde, Alhaji Suleiman worked so hard every day to achieve this and set an amazing example for everyone at the political arena.

He added that Suleiman has been blessed “with the team that surround you: The South West Coordinator, Mrs Ayodele Fatusin has been a pillar of strength and support for TSO in Nigeria and in Diaspora, she is a mother to all and we are blessed to have her. Hon. Akin Ibesanmi, the TSO Lagos State Coordinator has done extremely well. Hon Tayo Shodeinde, the TSO Diaspora Director is another excellent soldier of the TSO worldwide, just to mention but few.
We are happy that our principal Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu has emerged as the flag bearer for our great party APC. We rejoice and congratulate him.”

The TSO coordinator stated that Tinubu’s victory in the APC presidential primary is just one step on the journey, stressing that Suleiman’s ability to mobilise Nigerians home and abroad is the solution to solving Nigeria’s problems by ensuring that Bola Tinubu emerges victorious in the 2023 election.
