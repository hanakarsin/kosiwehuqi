Justin Perry - Age, Family, Bio

One of the names behind the brand YouAreCreators, a motivational and inspirational concept that informs of how every person creates their own reality. The idea is based on The Law of Attraction. The YouAreCreators YouTube channel has more than 1.7 million subscribers.

Before Fame

He launched the YouAreCreators YouTube channel in December of 2012.

Trivia

He authored the book I Wish I Knew This 20 Years Ago: Understanding The Universal Laws That Govern All Things. He also co-wrote books called 222 Prosperity Affirmations:: How To Speak Prosperity and Abundance into your life, The Little Book of Successful Secrets: What Successful People Know, But Don't Talk About and The Mind Of Money: The Blueprint Of Accumulating Wealth.

Family Life

His wife Ericka helps him with You Are Creators and launched her own company called The Stork Bag. They have three children.

Associated With

He and Kristen Butler both pass on their beliefs about the power of positivity and the law of attraction.
