Caroline Mutoko Says Indisciplined Kids Are a Result of Lazy Parenting: &quot;You Are the Authority&quot;

  • Radio veteran Caroline Mutoko has told parents to ensure they discipline their kids when they do wrong
  • The mom of two stated that her kids know she is the authority even when they want to throw a tantrum
  • The outspoken lady pointed out that disciplining children was a lot of work but has to be done

Media personality Caroline Mutoko has urged parents to discipline their kids to raise stable men and women in society.

The mother of two spoke over the weekend during an event dubbed Mums Who Rock, hosted by fellow media personality Amina Abdi.

Kids can smell fear

Mutoko said that it is important for parents whose kids are in the tantrums stage to nip it in the bud.

In a video shared by nana Owiti, the mom also warned those parents who insist on leaving kids alone to act out, saying they are too young for discipline.

"These kids are trying to push their boundaries, and it is okay. But you have to remember who is the adult in the situation. Kids can smell their parents fear," she explained.

The media personality shared personal experiences with parenting her kids, especially when they were at the tantrums stages.

Remember you are the authority

Mutoko said that she has constantly reminded her kids that she is the authority. Likewise, she urged parents always to remember that they are the authority in the situation.

"I used to tell Nduku 'you have 30 seconds to finish' when she would throw tantrums. She knows what it meant. I signed up for this job," she added.

Mutoko expressed her worry at the current parenting struggles and how people cringe at indisciplined kids.

The beautiful mom remarked that indisciplined children are simply a factor of lazy parenting because disciplining children is work.

Parents raising rats

She warned parents against waiting for kids to outgrow the tantrum stage, saying that's how people raise rats.

"There is no room for rats in my house," she noted adding that parents must give to society what they expect back.

She stated that she is raising her kids to be amazing people in society.

"You have to call out a man in your son, My son will grow to be an amazing young man not a grown male," she concluded.

Stop doing everything for your kids

The media personality recently reminded parents to stop doing everything for their kids.

While appearing on a YouTube channel interview with Kitt Kiarie, the radio queen pointed out that most parents do everything for their children that they feel entitled to be rescued by people when they grow up.

"Parenting is repeating until they get it, and you start early because you are creating a person," she added.
