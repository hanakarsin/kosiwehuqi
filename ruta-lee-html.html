Ruta Lee - Age, Family, Bio

Played Laura on the comedy drama Funny Bones alongside Oliver Platt.

Before Fame

She worked as a candy girl at Grauman's Chinese Theater.

Trivia

She was the co-host of the game show High Rollers with Alex Trebek.

Family Life

She married Webster B. Lowe Jr.

Associated With

She was in the TV film Sweet Bird of Youth with Elizabeth Taylor.
