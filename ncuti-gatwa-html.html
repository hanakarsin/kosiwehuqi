Ncuti Gatwa - Age, Family, Bio

Scottish actor who rose to fame by appearing on the Netflix series Sex Education in the role of Eric Effiong. He has also acted in the tv mini-series Stonemouth. In 2022, he won the role of the 15th incarnation of Doctor Who. 

Before Fame

One of his earliest acting gigs was in the 2014 TV mini-series Bob Servant. 

Trivia

He was featured in an edition of Teen Vogue in January 2019. He travelled to New York City in January 2019 as well. 

Family Life

He posted a photo with his mother to his Instagram account in December 2018. 

Associated With

He acted alongside Gillian Anderson in the TV series Sex Education.
