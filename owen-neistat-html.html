Owen Neistat - Age, Family, Bio

The son of writer and filmmaker Casey Neistat. His father rose to fame as one of the founders of the social media company Beme and the creator of HBO's The Neistat Brothers. 

Before Fame

His father was only seventeen when he was born.

Trivia

Through his family, he has inherited a substantial social media following which includes over 30,000 followers on Instagram. 

Family Life

His mother's name is Robin Harris. He has a younger half-sister named Francine.

Associated With

His stepmother is creative director and jewelry designer Candice Pool.
