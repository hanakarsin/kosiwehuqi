'Why I Killed My 23-Year-Old Nigerian Girlfriend

A 47-year-old Chinese national in Kano State, identified as Geng Quangrong, has disclosed why he stabbed his girlfriend, Ummu Kulthum, to death in Kabuga Quarters, Dorayi Babba area of the State.

Naija News reports that the suspect, who was arrested after he attacked and killed his 23-year-old girlfriend in her home on Friday night, 16th September, said he did it because he was betrayed.

According to the state Police Public Relations Officer, Abdullahi Haruna Kiyawa, the suspect invaded the deceased home after scaling the fence to gain access and repeatedly stabbed her until she passed away.

Geng alleged that Ummu had promised to marry him, but later reneged on the promise after he had spent so much money on her.

The suspect further claimed he was angered by Ummmu’s deception, and he decided to attack and kill her at her residence.

Deng has since been transferred to the State Police headquarters at Bompai for further investigation and prosecution while Ummu was buried on Saturday morning, 17th September.

He Once Attempted Suicide

A neighbour to Ummu, Abubakar Mustapha who spoke with Daily Trust revealed that she was a divorcee and the suspect was her boyfriend.

Mustapha said, “The incident happen around 10:00 pm last night. The murderer was her boyfriend since before she got married. Her husband divorced her and they went on with their friendship as boyfriend and girlfriend. He often went to her house. She was taken to UMC Zhair Hospital not more than a kilometre away from her house and she died.

“Khaleed, her brother stopped the mob from attacking the murderer when he came back for his car when he entered the house because he (Khaleed) thought that will be lead to a different thing. The man was then caught and handed over to the police.”

Another neighbour, Muhammad Sani, noted that the Chinese man once attempted suicide because of his girlfriend, adding that the matter was settled with the intervention of the police.

Sani said, “When she was about to get married, he once attempted suicide because of his love for her until the police intervened and everything was settled.”
