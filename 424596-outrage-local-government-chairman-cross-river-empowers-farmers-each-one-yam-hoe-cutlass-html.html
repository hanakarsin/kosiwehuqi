Outrage as Local Government Chairman Empowers Farmers Each with One Yam, Jembe

  • The chairman of Obanliku local government area of Cross River State, Evangelist Margaret Inde, has got people talking on social media
  • Inde has come under fire for sharing a jembe, cutlass and one tuber of yam with farmers as a way to empower them
  • These items were shared by the chairman during the New Yam festival held on Friday, August 27
  • Many who shared their thoughts on the development wondered why the farmers were comfortable receiving such items as empowerment

The chairman of Obanliku local government area of Cross River, Evangelist Margaret Inde, has empowered farmers with a hoe, a cutlass and one tuber of yam each.

According to Sahara Reporters, the farmers received the items during activities to mark the 30th anniversary of the birth of the local government area as well as a celebration of the New Yam festival held on Friday, August 27.
