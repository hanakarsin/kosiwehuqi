Singer Akothee officially opens foundation in Turkana

In less than a year, singer Akothee has proved that deep within her lies a kind heart and bright soul.

The mother of five has managed to do what most leaders could never dream of using her hard earned money.

In a sweet Instagram post, the single mother announced she had officially opened and launched her foundation in Turkana county that would help cater for needy and hunger stricken residents.

At 4am, the musician was already up and ready to fly to Turkana.

Hours later, she announced her act of kindness had just begun.

Popularly referred to as the president of single mothers, Akothee added some of her clients were touched by her work and they also wanted to travel to Turkana in a bid to help alleviate people's suffering.

A few photos shared on the singer's social media proved all she does is not for show.

Instead, the mother of five pours her heart and soul in her philanthropic works to a point she becomes one with the people she helps.

As earlier reported by TUKO.co.ke, the gorgeous musician promised to sink six boreholes in Turkana.

This came after she was pictured distributing food supplies and water tanks to the poverty stricken residents who smiled all through the exercise.

Akothee even rallied up funds from her fans and purchased supplies for Turkana residents when reports emerged the area had been rendered dry and that famine had ravaged most homes.
