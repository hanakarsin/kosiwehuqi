Kenyans react to Kirinyaga Governor Anne Waiguru's impeachment: &quot;No more catwalking&quot;

- Kirinyaga MCAs passed a motion to impeach embattled Kirinyaga Governor Anne Waiguru in the afternoon of Tuesday, June 9, in a stormy session

- Before voting took place to remove her, chaos had earlier erupted after two of her allies MCAs, Anthony Munene (Karumandi Ward) and nominated MCA Lucy Njeri, were kicked out

- In President Uhuru Kenyatta's first term in 2015, Waiguru resigned as Devolution CS after pressure from Kenyans over corruption allegations

Kenyans have taken to social media by storm to react to Governor Anne Waiguru's impeachment by the Kirinyaga County Assembly.

Waiguru, a first-term governor and one among the only two women county leaders, was ousted from her plum position in the afternoon of Tuesday, June 9, after 23 out of 33 MCAs voted to kick her out.

The MCAs accused her of gross misconduct and abuse of office.

Here was how netizens reacted under the #WaiguruImpeached.

"Governor Waiguru impeached! It may be a tall order to remove the high priestess of corruption, but the message is clear. No more cat-walking! a netizen using @Turkana_Oil username tweeted.

"Ruto just pulled a fast one on Uhuru. Hitting them where it hurts," Kenneth Ndegwa opined."Waiguru has been impeached by Kirinyaga MCAs. Kibicho working in silence,"Enock Njau said."I concur with Kirinyaga Governor Anne Waiguru's doctor. "Anne Waiguru must to go," Peter Ng'etich added.

Before voting took place to remove her, chaos had earlier erupted after two of her allies MCAs Anthony Munene (Karumandi Ward) and nominated MCA Lucy Njeri were kicked out of the proceedings.

It was not the first time for Waiguru to be hounded out of office.
