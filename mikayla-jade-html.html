Mikayla Jade - Age, Family, Bio

YouTuber turned singer who became famous for her OfficialMikaaylaa YouTube channel, where she uploaded covers of hit pop songs. In 2017, she released her original feature titled "Invitation" and a year later she released her first single titled "Breaking You Off."

Before Fame

Her father was in a hip hop band growing up and became her main inspiration for music. She was featured in a few of his albums and grew up around the music scene. She joined YouTube in late 2009 and uploaded her first video, "'Use Somebody' Cover," in February of 2010.

Trivia

She has earned more than 60,000 YouTube subscribers and more than 4.5 million individual views.

Family Life

She was born in New Haven, Connecticut and raised in Florida. She has siblings named Amaiya Joy and Isaiah Jace. 

Associated With

In one of her most popular videos she shared impressions of Demi Lovato, Britney Spears, and Ellie Goulding.
