'It Is A Sin' - Fans Fault Peter Okoye 'Unusual' Air Kiss With James Brown

Some fans of Nigerian singer, Peter Okoye better known as Mr P have faulted his mannerisms while exchanging pleasantries with fast- rising cross dresser, James Brown. 

Naija News reports that the singer was spotted at the 40th birthday of Nollywood actress, Ini Edo exchanging pleasantries with James Brown in an usual way, thereby causing a stir amongst fans online.

In the video that has surfaced on social media, Peter Okoye was seen exchanging an air kiss with James Brown as they exchanged pleasantries.

Reacting to the video, a fan identified sandra_benneth questioned the singer on the reason why he greeted the cross dresser in such a manner.

She wrote: “Why peter dey greet am like say he dey greet woman identified as  faulted the singer for greeting the cross dresser as though he was a woman.”

Another fan, emarh_jerry wrote; ”But this is wrong tho….It’s a stated sin in the Bible,but these days everyone be taking it as a normz. This is devilish”

One vitamin_dharmie wrote; ‘‘Why men dey hug each other like that ? Like? WT heck???”
