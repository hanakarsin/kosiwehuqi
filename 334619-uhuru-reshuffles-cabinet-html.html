Uhuru fires Mwangi Kiunjuri in new Cabinet reshuffle

- President Uhuru named new and replaced some members of his Cabinet

- Foreign Affairs CS Monica Juma was swapped by Raychelle Omamo

- Amina Mohammed, Aden Mohammed, Balala and Wamalwa retained their Cabinet posts

- Kiunjuri, an ardent Deputy President William Ruto's supporter, was holding the Agriculture docket which was handed to Peter Munya

President Uhuru Kenyatta has announced critical changes in his Cabinet.

Speaking during a televised address at State House Lodge in Mombasa on Tuesday, January 14, Uhuru relieved Agriculture Cabinet Secretary Mwangi Kiunjuri of his duties and replaced him with Peter Munya who was in charge of Trade.

He also swapped Monica Juma with Raychelle Omamo. Monica will now be in charge of the Defence docket while Omamo was moved to Foreign Affairs.

East African Community and Regional Development Cabinet Secretary Aden Mohammed and his Devolution colleague Eugene Wamalwa as well as Najib Balala (Tourism) were not affected by the changes.

Amina Mohammed also retained her Sports Cabinet post.

Former Nyeri senator Mutahi Kagwe will now head the Health docket as Sicily Kariuki who moves from Afya House to the Ministry of Water and Irrigation.

The immediate Water and Irrigation CS Simon Chelugui will take over from Ukuru Yattani who has fully been confirmed Treasury boss.

Until today, Yattani was the acting Treasury Cabinet secretary following suspension of Henry Rotich.

Kiunjuri's stint was met with heavy criticism from Uhuru and the public over the manner in which he ran the affairs of one of the biggest ministries especially maize farmers.

The matter came to the fore during Mashujaa Day celebrations in Kakamega county in October 2018.

Advancing his scathing attack to Kiunjuri, Uhuru said he would not accept to face the wrath of farmers again when the government had played its part of disbursing funds for their produce.

"The ball is in your court Kiunjuri. You need to ensure all those thieves are arrested and prosecuted or else you will pay the price for their sins," Uhuru said.

The fired CS is also a strong supporter of Deputy President William Ruto and his Tanga Tanga brigade.
