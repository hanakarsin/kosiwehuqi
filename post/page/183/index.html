Court Vacates Re-Arrest Order Against Jaruma

Recall that the self-acclaimed sex therapist was arrested and charged to court over defamation of character, false news publication and intimidation. The former lawmaker representing Aniocha North-South and Oshimili North-South, Ned Nwoko had submitted a petition against her.
