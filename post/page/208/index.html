Soni Nicole Bringas - Age, Family, Bio

published on April 12, 2024
Actress who was cast as Ramona Gibbler on the Netflix series Fuller House and also appeared in the Lifetime movie Beautiful & Twisted. She initially gained fame as a dancer, working with such choreographers as Matt Steffanina, Willdabeast, Nappytabs, Marty Kudelka, Tricia Miranda and Nick Demoura. Before Fame She started dancing when she was 5 years old. By the time she was a teenager, she had already worked in commercials and was dancing competitively. Trivia She had a role as a hip-hop dancer in the CW's Jane the Virgin and has made appearances in several commercials, including American Girl, the Jordache commercial with Heidi Klum and the Swagger Wagon Toyota commercial.
