List of all Airtel call offers in 2020

Over the last couple of months, consumers are increasingly focusing on cutting down their expenses, and they want to spend considerably less money on making calls. If you are looking for a service provider that offers the most affordable call rates in the country, check out the latest Airtel call offers in 2020.

Airtel Kenya is the second-largest telecommunication service provider in Kenya after Safaricom. It covers about 80% of the population, and it focuses its efforts on increasing coverage across all parts of the country. Since it began its operations in 2010, Airtel Kenya has endeavoured to bring communication costs to affordable levels by introducing value for money products and services.

Airtel minutes offers

In line with its mission to offer the most affordable call rates to its clients, Airtel Kenya has rolled out exciting call minutes offers. If you have an Airtel line, you can choose either the Airtel Tubonge or Airtel UnlimiNET talk time packs.

Airtel Tubonge

Airtel Tubonge is one of the most exciting offers that is set to make your life a lot simpler. The prepaid package targets Kenyans from all walks of life that rely on making calls as their preferred mode of communication.

Tubonge subscription allows you to make Airtel to Airtel calls at no extra charge and a preferential rate of 2 Shillings + Tax to call other networks. There are 3 Tubonge offers:

  • A daily offer for Kshs. 10
  • A weekly offer for Kshs. 50
  • A monthly offer of Kshs. 150

All Tubonge offers come with 100mins for Airtel to Airtel calls per day. To opt-in on the Tubonge offer, dial *544*10# from your Airtel line and follow the prompts. Remember to top up your line with airtime if you wish to make calls to other networks.

Airtel UnlimiNET

If you have an Airtel line, you can also buy the daily UNlimiNET 20, 50, or 100 packs to enjoy free minutes when making Airtel to Airtel calls or when calling other networks. With the UnlimiNet 50 pack, you get to enjoy 10 minutes Airtel to Airtel talk time and 10 Minutes talk time to other networks.

Airtel to Airtel free calls in Kenya

Are you stuck on how to get free calls on Airtel? Tubonge Plus is an exciting package that allows Airtel customers to make Airtel to Airtel free calls. There are two options that customers can choose from:

  • Tubonge Plus (voice) - At the cost of Kshs. 20, you get to enjoy free Airtel to Airtel calls. You are also given free 10 minutes talk time from your Airtel line to other networks. The validity of the bundle package is 24 hours.
  • Tubonge Plus (combo) - At the cost of Kshs. 20, you get to enjoy free Airtel to Airtel calls. If you wish to call other networks, you will be charged 2 bob + tax, but you must top up your Airtel line. You also get to enjoy free 50Mbs data when you choose this offer.

Airtel call rates to other networks

Prepaid customers on the Airtel network can call other networks at the rate of 2 bob per minute. The introduction of the 2 bob is a great milestone that has been achieved in Airtel's journey of seeking to offer customers affordable calling rates to other networks.
