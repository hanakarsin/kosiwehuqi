Nadia Mukami Gets Fans Talking after Displaying Wards of Dollars Hours after US Show

  • Nadia Mukami has been in the US for music shows and performance
  • The musician shared a video of herself showing off a ward of dollars as she thanked her fans for attending her show
  • She noted she was looking forward to her next show set for Kansas city

Kenyan songstress Nadia Mukami has impressed and inspired millions of her fans after showcasing the fruits of her labour.

The musician who is currently on a tour in the United States of America on Monday, September 6, shared a video of herself flaunting wards of dollars.

Taking to her Instagram account, the star shared the video and thanked the people of Houston for making it possible for her to make such huge amounts of money.

Thank you, Houston

Nadia thanked people who came through for her show and even showered her with notes midway through her show.

In the caption, Nadia disclosed she was looking forward to her next show in the US which will be held in Kansas.

"Houston thank you for making it rain for me on stage! Next stop Kansas Let’s Go!" Nadia wrote.

Her video impressed her social media followers and fans who camped at the comments section of his post to congratulate and wish her all the best in her career.

Recently, TUKO.co.ke reported that Nadia took to social media to celebrate and thank God after her collaboration song with Sanaipei Tande, Wangu, hit the 10 million views mark on YouTube.

Celebrating the win, Nadia took to Instagram and shared the good news with her fans while thanking them for making it possible.

In a post seen by TUKO.co.ke, Nadia noted that Wangu video was the first of her many songs to hit 10 million views on the online platform.
