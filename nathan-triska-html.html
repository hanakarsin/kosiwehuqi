Nathan Triska - Age, Family, Bio

Social media celebrity who first became known for his live broadcasts on YouNow and for posting Vine videos on his channel ThatWhiteKid. He has over 1 million followers on Instagram where he primarily posts shots of himself with different fashion looks set against a range of backdrops. 

Before Fame

He began his social media career as early as 2012 when he first signed up for Twitter. 

Trivia

He portrays the character Frankie on the web series Chicken Girls. He is popular on TikTok with over 10 million fans on his nathantriska account. 

Family Life

He was born in Seattle, Washington. His friend Joey Caster often affectionately refers to him as his little brother. He was married in July 2024.

Associated With

He and fellow social star Mark Thomas have collaborated on posts together.
