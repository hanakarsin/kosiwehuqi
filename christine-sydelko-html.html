Christine Sydelko - Age, Family, Bio

Social media personality known for collaborating with Elijah Daniel on the YouTube channel Elijah & Christine. She also has a self-titled vlogging channel, which has earned more than 1.1 million subscribers. Her "BING BONG (OFFICIAL MUSIC VIDEO) - Closer by The Chainsmokers & Halsey PARODY" video has received over 4 million views since it was posted in January of 2017.

Before Fame

She enrolled at DePaul University, where she studied digital media and cinema. She began creating content for YouTube in July of 2015. 

Trivia

She is also popular on Instagram, with more than 600,000 followers, and she gained a substantial following on Vine, finishing with more than 500,000 followers by the time the app was shut down. She has posted multiple anti-conservative memes on her Instagram page. 

Family Life

She is from Chicago, Illinois. In 2015, she dropped out of college to move to Los Angeles, California. She grew up with two older brothers. 

Associated With

She has worked with fellow social stars Tom Harlock and Erin Gilfoy.
