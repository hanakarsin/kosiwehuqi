Buhari Arrives In London For Medical Check-up (Video)

The President of Nigeria, Muhammadu Buhari has arrived in London, United Kingdom for his scheduled medical check-up, Naija News reports.

Presidential media aide, Femi Adesina had on Monday announced the planned medical trip in a statement made available to newsmen.

Adesina said the medical trip is a routine one and Buhari is expected back in Nigeria in the second week of November.

He had written: “President Muhammadu Buhari leaves for London, United Kingdom, October 31, 2022, for routine medical check-up.

“He returns to the country in the second week of November, 2022.”

An update was provided on Tuesday by the President’s Special Assistant on Digital Communications, Bashir Ahmad who confirmed that Buhari has landed in London.

Ahmad also reiterated that the President is on a routine medical check-up and is expected back in the country the second week of November.

He uploaded a video on Twitter in the early hours of Tuesday with the caption: “President Muhammadu Buhari arrives London, United Kingdom, for routine medical check-up. The President to return to the country in the second week of November, 2022.”
