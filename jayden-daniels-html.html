Jayden Daniels (TV Actor) - Age, Family, Bio

Kiwi actor who earned his big break playing Curtis Hannah in the long-running soap opera Shortland Street. In 2023, he played Gabriel in the horror movie Evil Dead Rise. He has appeared in more than 400 episodes of television throughout his ongoing career. 

Before Fame

He made his first screen appearance in the 2012 short film Decisions. 

Trivia

In 2015, he played Tyler in the television show End of Term. His acting fame has led him to amass over 60,000 followers on his jaydendaniels__ Instagram page. 

Family Life

His mother's name is Lisa Tangira. He has a sister named Chantelle. 

Associated With

On the New Zealand series Shortland Street, he stars alongside Amanda Billing and Angela Bloomfield.
