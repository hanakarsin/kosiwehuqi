Courtney Miller - Age, Family, Bio

Writer and actress for Smosh who first gained fame on Vine, earning more than 200,000 followers by the time the app was shut down. Her YouTube channel has featured multiple compilations of the videos that were previously on her Vine. 

Before Fame

She began editing, writing, and shooting her own movies when she was 10 years old. 

Trivia

She posts acoustic song covers on Snapchat. She has more than 330,000 subscribers on her own YouTube channel. She dressed up as Logan Paul in a picture on her Instagram from February of 2021. 

Family Life

She grew up with six siblings in a small town outside of Malibu, California.

Associated With

She collaborated with such Viners as Brandon Calvillo, Jake Moreno, and Alex Ernst. She's also worked with Ian Hecox and Anthony Padilla on Smosh videos.
