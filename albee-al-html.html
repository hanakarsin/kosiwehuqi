Albee Al - Age, Family, Bio

American rapper who has earned buzz with mixtapes like 2010's Somethin da Hood Made and those in his Everyday June 30th series. Some of his most popular tracks include "Shining Down," "I"m a Gee" and "Getting to the Cake."

Before Fame

He grew up in Jersey City, New Jersey.

Trivia

He suffered a gunshot wound to his right arm.

Family Life

His real name is Albert Robinson.

Associated With

He collaborated with Remy Boy Monty on "Getting to the Cake."
