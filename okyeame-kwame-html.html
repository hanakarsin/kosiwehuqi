Okyeame Kwame - Age, Family, Bio

Ghanaian rapper also known as Rap Doctor who rose to prominence in the 1990's as a member of the hiplife music duo Akyeame, who released albums like Shackles and New Testament. Following the band's breakup, he broke out as a solo artist with 2004's Promised Child.

Before Fame

He attended Anglican Senior High School in Kumasi, Ghana. He later studied classical guitar at Kwame Nkrumah University.

Trivia

He won a slew of awards at the 2009 Ghana Music Awards, including Artist of the Year, Hip-Hop Song of the Year and Best Music Video of the Year.

Family Life

His real name is Kwame Nsiah Apau. He is the third of six siblings, two of which have performed as the musical duo Bradez. He married Annica Nsiah-Apau, with whom he has children named Bota and Sante.

Associated With

He and Bollie Babeface are both known for playing the Ghanaian subgenre of music known as hiplife.
