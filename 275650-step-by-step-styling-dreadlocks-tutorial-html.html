Step by step barrel twist style for dreadlocks

One of the popular dreadlocks styling techniques is the barrel twist. The hairstyle is perfect for long, short, and medium dreadlocks. The style is just like twisting your hair. Below are the steps to follow when plaiting barrel twists.

  • Partition your dreadlocks
  • Select one section of the partitioned dreads, then take the first loc strand and twist it.
  • Take two more locs and twist them together to form a one big braid
  • Then select one loc on the side and twist it into the braid and wrap it underneath
  • Take another loc this time on the other side then twist it over the braid.
  • Repeat the fourth and fifth steps until all locs are done
  • When you are at the ends, take the longest loc and wrap it around the braid and repeat this step until the end
  • Secure the twist by tying a knot using the locs at the end.
  • If your dreadlocks are long enough, you can hold the ends in a bun
