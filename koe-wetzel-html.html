Koe Wetzel - Age, Family, Bio

Singer and guitarist best known as the leader of country music group Koe Wetzel and the Konvicts. The group released their debut album Out on Parole in 2015 before releasing their breakthrough record, Noise Complaint, in 2017.

Before Fame

He lived in Pittsburg, Texas before moving to Stephenville. He attended Tarleton State University.

Trivia

His song "February 28th, 2016" became the most listened-to country song in several Texas cities in 2017, including Richardson, Plano and Arlington.

Family Life

He has two younger sisters.

Associated With

He has covered the Jason Aldean song "The Truth."
