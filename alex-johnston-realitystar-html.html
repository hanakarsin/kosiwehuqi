Alex Johnston (Reality Star) - Age, Family, Bio

Member of the popular Johnston family whe joined his parents and siblings on the TLC reality series 7 Little Johnstons. 

Before Fame

He debuted on 7 Little Johnstons in March of 2015 with his parents Amber and Trent Johnston. 

Trivia

After he was diagnosed with central apnea, he had to undergo brain surgery to correct the problem. 

Family Life

The fourth of the Johnston family, he was adopted by his parents from Seoul, South Korea. 

Associated With

He is joined on 7 Little Johnstons by his siblings Jonah, Anna, Elizabeth, and Emma.
