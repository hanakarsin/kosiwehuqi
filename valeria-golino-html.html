Valeria Golino - Age, Family, Bio

Italian beauty who was featured in many films in Italy and appeared in Hot Shots! and Rain Man. In 2009, she won an Athens Panorama Special Award for Acting.

Before Fame

She modeled in Athens and left high school to pursue a career as an actress.

Trivia

The highlight of her career may have come in 1986 when she won the award for Best Actress at the acclaimed Venice Film Festival.

Family Life

She was in a relationship with actor Riccardo Scamarcio from 2006 to 2017. 

Associated With

She played the girlfriend of Tom Cruise in the classic film Rain Man.
