Hailey Williams - Age, Family, Bio

Best known for her lip sync videos alongside her best friend Madi Sweet, posted to the joint madihaileyofficial TikTok account, which has earned over 220,000 fans. She has posted an advertisement for the The Coldest Water bottle company on her personal hhaileywilliams TikTok. 

Before Fame

She grew up playing sports year round, including basketball, volleyball, and soccer. She began posting videos to her personal TikTok account, iamhailey42, in 2015 before joining Madi in 2016 to start madihaileyofficial. She later changed her personal account to hhaileywilliams.

Trivia

She and Madi split all of their social media accounts, including Twitter, YouNow, Instagram, and YouTube. A dance video they posted to the "Feel it Still" song by Portugal. The Man has received over 83 thousand hearts. 

Family Life

She was born in Eugene, Oregon; but grew up in Springfield, Oregon. She has an older sister, Jasmyne, and an older brother, Dakota. 

Associated With

She and Madi put together a collaborative musical.ly account called BombSquad_ along with Max and Harvey Mills.
