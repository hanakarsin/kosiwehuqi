RawBeautyKristi - Age, Family, Bio

Beauty vlogger on YouTube, she makes product reviews, tutorials for regular looks and special effects make up, and vlogs about life, love, and more. 

Before Fame

She stresses that she is a self-taught make up artist and not a professional one. She joined YouTube and began making her videos in 2013. 

Trivia

She attended the NYX Face Awards in 2015 to support many of her friends, who were nominated or won awards. 

Family Life

She got married to her husband Zack in 2010. In 2020, they welcomed their first child, a son. She has a cat named Turkey.

Associated With

She became extremely good friends with make up artist and Instagram and YouTube sensation Glam&Gore.
