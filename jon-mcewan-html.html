Jon McEwan - Age, Family, Bio

Rose to fame as the fourth husband of Coronation Street star Beverley Callard. The couple married in a ceremony in Eccles, Manchester, England in 2010.

Before Fame

He served in the British Army. His first date with Beverley Callard was set up as a blind date.

Trivia

He has appeared on the British talk show Loose Women. He was often talked about by his wife during her time on I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here in 2020.

Family Life

He has two step-children with Beverley Callard. He has a son named Ben.

Associated With

His wife appeared alongside Giovanna Fletcher during the 2020 season of I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here.
