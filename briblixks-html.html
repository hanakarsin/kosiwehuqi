Briblixks - Age, Family, Bio

Trending dance and lip-sync content creator who rose to fame by sharing performances on her self-titled TikTok account. She has more than 5 million dedicated fans on the platform. She has also posted some of her videos to her thebriblixks Instagram account.

Before Fame

She made her TikTok debut in May of 2020. The video was a POV of an argument with a boyfriend.

Trivia

One of her most popular dance performances was posted in late 2020. It has been viewed more than 600,000 times. Her dance videos are often set to trendy hip-hop songs. 

Family Life

She was born in the Bronx, New York and is based in Connecticut. 

Associated With

In July of 2021, she performed a lip-sync to music by rapper $not.
