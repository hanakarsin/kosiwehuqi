The punches just keep coming for Ryan Garcia.

It has been reported that the professional boxer tested positive for performance-enhancing drugs. This comes on the heels of his win against opponent Devin Haney. His dating life has been another source of controversy.

He has been linked to several women since his divorce from Andrea Celina, the mother of his two children, Bela and Henry Leo.

Who is Ryan Garcia dating? Keep reading to find out.

Who is Ryan Garcia dating?

The boxing star has been photographed with several beautiful women following his win against Haney. Among them is Grace Boor, a model and a regular on the pageant circuit.

She is also a social media influencer — she has nearly 3 million followers on Instagram alone.

The pair were recently photographed holding hands as they hung out on a beach in Miami. However, let's be clear — dating and being in a relationship are two very different things.

After Ryan and Grace hung out, TMZ got the scoop on their status.

"This was our first time seeing each other in person and we just hit it off," Boor said. "It was just like an instant click." But intense chemistry wasn't enough for Grace to consider him as boyfriend material.

She explained that she turned Ryan down after he asked her out.

"I said, 'I can't do that,'" Boor added. "Because he has, like, all these girls and stuff and I'm not about it."

Grace continued, "So, maybe if he changed, maybe the answer would change. But, I don't know what's going on and that's his business."

Their relationship status may be undecided right now, but Grace wasn't shy to defend Ryan when it came to recent drug allegations.

As a quick refresher, Ryan tested positive for the performance-enhancing drug ostarine on the day before and after his fight with Haney.

"Everybody knows that I don't cheat," Ryan said in a video posted on Twitter.

"Never taken a steroid. ... I don't even know where to get steroids. ... I barely take supplements. Big lies, I beat his a--."

Grace defended Ryan by captioning a photo of them in Miami with, “@kingryan DID NOT CHEAT.”

Ryan Garcia has been linked to numerous other women.

Ryan may have a few other women on his roster. And no surprise here: they’re all knockouts. He reportedly proposed to Australian adult film worker, Savannah Bond, beforehis recent fight.

“SHE SAID YESS!!!!!!. I'M SO HAPPY AND MOTIVATED GOING INTO MY FIGHT NOW,” he wrote on Twitter.

However, Ryan admitted to TMZ that it was all fake and he was just caught up in the moment.

He has also been recently linked to Alexa Dellanos, the daughter of TV host, Myrka Dellanos.

Needless to say, Ryan has plenty of women to pick from. Although he doesn't seem to be dating or ready to settle down with anyone in particular right now, that can always change. We wish him all the best in his personal and professional endeavors. It remains to be seen what comes of Ryan's positive drug test and how that will impact his career moving forward.
