Maxmello - Age, Family, Bio

Widely known as the partner of YouTube beauty and lifestyle sensation Wengie. In August 2015, his then engagement to her was announced on her Instagram account. He has also received collectively over 15 million views on his "Real VS Fake Challenge" videos on YouTube. 

Before Fame

He met Wengie while both were college students. He was in the same classes as she was and began talking to her there.

Trivia

He has operated his own ReactiCorns YouTube channel, where he often reacts to pop culture videos. He has amassed more than 2.1 million subscribers there. He also created his own "Animal Island" Roblox server. 

Family Life

He was formally engaged to Wengie, and the couple often featured one another on their YouTube channels.

Associated With

He and Wengie posted a vocal cover of Zayn's hit single "Pillowtalk" to her WengieVlogs channel in April 2016.
