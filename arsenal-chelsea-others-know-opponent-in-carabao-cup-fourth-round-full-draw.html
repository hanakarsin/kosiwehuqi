Arsenal, Chelsea, Others Know Opponent In Carabao Cup Fourth Round [Full Draw]

The Carabao Cup fourth round draw was conducted after matches were played on Wednesday night.

Champions League winners, Chelsea which qualified for the next stage after defeating Aston Villa on penalties will clash with Southampton next at Stamford Bridge.

North London side, Arsenal will play against Leeds, while Stoke will clash with Brentford.

West Ham which defeated Manchester United will play against Manchester City.

Liverpool will face Preston in the next round.

See the full fixture below:

Chelsea v Southampton

Arsenal v Leeds

Stoke v Brentford

West Ham v Man City

Leicester v Brighton

Burnley v Tottenham

QPR v Sunderland

Preston v Liverpool
