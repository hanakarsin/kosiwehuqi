Why Nigerians Should Not Vote For Me

The presidential candidate of the Labour Party (LP), Peter Obi, had urged Nigerians not to vote for him in next year’s election based on ethnic or entitlement sentiments.

Speaking on Sunday during the presidential Town Hall series organized by Arise TV, the former Anambra State Governor said Nigerians should not vote for him because ‘it is his turn’, but on the competence he comes with.

Emi lokan

While stating that it is nobody’s turn but that of the youth and the women, Obi said he has the capacity to take Nigeria out of its current predicament and Nigerians should back him on that basis.

“Do not vote for Peter Obi because I am from the South East, because I am a Christian, because I am an Igbo man, or because it is my turn. It is turn of Nigeria Youth and Women, It is nobody’s turn.

“I want you to vote for Peter Obi because I am qualified. Because I am competent. I know what I can do to put this country out of the brink,” he said.

Insecurity In Nigeria

Obi stressed the need to strengthen and revive the local vigilante system in the country and have them work together with the police to solve Nigeria’s security challenges

He stated that he will provide for every community a vigilante system that is working, provide them with vehicles, communication equipment, linking them to work with police.
