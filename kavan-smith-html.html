Kavan Smith - Age, Family, Bio

Canadian actor known best for his roles as Major Evan Lorne in the television show, Stargate Atlantis and Agent Jed Garrity in the television series, The 4400.

Before Fame

He attended the University of Calgary majoring in Economics and dropped out after a year to pursue acting at Mount Royal University.

Trivia

He took martial arts classes for 15 years.

Family Life

He has one brother and they were both raised by his father after their parents divorced when he was a child. He and his wife have two children.

Associated With

He appeared as Nicholas Willis in the 2000 film, Misson to Mars starring Tim Robbins.
