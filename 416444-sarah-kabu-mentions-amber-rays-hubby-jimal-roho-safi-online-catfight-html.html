Sarah Kabu Says She Dislikes Amber Ray's Hubby Jimal Roho Safi

Old habits die hard, a famous saying goes. Just a month after socialite Amber Ray told Bonfire Adventures’ Managing Director Sarah Kabu to stop sticking her nose in her business, the businesswoman is back at it again.

Via a Q and A session on Instagram, one of her followers inquired whether her husband Simon Kabu has friends she worries about.

While responding to the fan, Sarah said that she dislikes Amber’s hubby Jimal Roho Safi.

In a since-deleted post on her Instagram stories, Sarah said she distastes him because of his approach to marriage.

“Please advise if there are some of your husband's friends who give you shivers,” the fan wrote. "I don’t like this guy. He has different values on marriage and public display," Sarah wrote and tagged Jimal.
