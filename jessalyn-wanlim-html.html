Jessalyn Wanlim - Age, Family, Bio

Film and television actress who first rose to fame portraying Rachael in All My Children. She then went on to become Giselle Vickers in Disney's Lab Rats and Evie Cho in the fourth season of BBC America's Orphan Black. 

Before Fame

She began performing early in life when she began singing in music school. She then ventured into stage acting during a high school art course.

Trivia

In 2010, she brought to life Patty Hong in the ABC sitcom Scoundrels.

Family Life

Her mother migrated from the Philippines to Canada after she married her father, a Chinese-American.

Associated With

In 2012, she starred opposite Tyler Perry in the action film Alex Cross.
