LunarEclipse - Age, Family, Bio

Sketch artist, illustrator and graphic designer primarily known for her speed art and speed paint videos on YouTube where she has amassed over 1.1 million subscribers.

Before Fame

She joined YouTube in August of 2015 but didn't start posting videos until 2017.

Trivia

She can be found on Instagram under the username lunareclispe, where she also shares her sketches. 

Family Life

Her real name is Wenny. Her sisters are YouTubers ItsFunneh, GoldenGlare and PaintingRainbows and her brother is YouTuber Draconitedragon. 

Associated With

She designed the YouTube banner for her sister ItsFunneh.
