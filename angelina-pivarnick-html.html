Angelina Pivarnick - Age, Family, Bio

Original cast member of Jersey Shore who was replaced by Deena Nicole Cortese in season 2. In 2016, she became a registered EMT in New York. 

Before Fame

She worked as a waitress and brought her clothing in garbage bags to the shore house on the series' first episode.

Trivia

She appeared in an episode of VH1's Couples Therapy with her previous boyfriend.

Family Life

She grew up in Staten Island, and is of Italian and Polish ancestry. She married Chris Larangeira in 2019. 

Associated With

She often got into verbal and physical altercations with Nicole 'Snooki' Polizzi.
