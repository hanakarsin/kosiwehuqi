Inbar Lavi - Age, Family, Bio

Israeli actress known for her role as Raviva on the MTV program Underemployed and Eve in Netflix's Lucifer. She has also appeared in episodes of such popular series as CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, The Closer, and Entourage. In 2017, she began starring in the Bravo series Imposters. 

Before Fame

After training at Tel Aviv's Sofi Moskowitz School of Acting, she moved to New York City and performed in off-Broadway stage productions before earning a scholarship to attend the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute in Los Angeles.

Trivia

Beginning in 2014, she played the role of Veronica Hicks on the American television series Gang Related.

Family Life

During her childhood in Ramat Gan, Israel, she battled asthma, spending numerous days at home watching and falling in love with various films.

Associated With

As a child, she was inspired by the career of fellow Israeli-born actress Natalie Portman.
