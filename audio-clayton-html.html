Audio Clayton - Age, Family, Bio

Son of actress Shannyn Sossamon and author Dallas Clayton. His name is short for Audio Science. 

Before Fame

His father wrote a 2009 children's picture book for him titled An Awesome World!.

Trivia

His father and singer/songwriter Sia co-wrote the screenplay for the 2021 film Music.

Family Life

He has a younger brother named Mortimer Kaufman.

Associated With

His mother has worked with actors such as Heath Ledger and Josh Hartnett.
