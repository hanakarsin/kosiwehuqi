Kind Bus Driver Returns Bag Containing KSh 4.4 Million and Other Valuables to Owner at The Airport

  • An honest Nigerian driver, Emmanuel Eluu, has earned praise after returning KSh 4.4 million and other valuables
  • The bundles of money were left in his bus by one of the passengers he served at the Murtala Muhammed Airport in Lagos
  • Airport officials have since returned the cash to the Port Harcourt-bound passenger

Emmanuel Eluu, a Nigerian bus driver who ferries passengers from the Murtala Muhammed Airport, has been praised for returning KSh 4.4 million left in his vehicle.

Channels TV reports that Eluu's act of kindness was disclosed in a statement by Bi-Courtney Aviation Services Limited (BASL) on Wednesday, September 1.

Honest man

The media outlet reported that Eluu found pieces of jewellery and other valuables worth millions in the vehicle aside from the money.

The statement also revealed the number plate of the vehicle the honest driver operates. The money was returned to the owner.
