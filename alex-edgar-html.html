Alex Edgar - Age, Family, Bio

Born Alex Edgar and better known by his online alter ego "PauseUnpause," this Canadian web star is famous for his YouTube gaming videos. His YouTube channel has earned more than 192 million views. 

Before Fame

Known primarily as a multi-player Minecraft gamer, he joined the popular video game community's MindCrack server in late 2011. 

Trivia

His most popular YouTube video, "Hunger Games" has over 5.4 million views.

Family Life

He grew up in Winnipeg, Manitoba and Duncan, British Columbia in a family of Canadian Aboriginal heritage. In 2014, he married his girlfriend of many years, a fellow gamer named Andrea.

Associated With

He has featured gameplay clips from Rocket League on his Twitch channel.
