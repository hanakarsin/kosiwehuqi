'Ms. Marvel' Latest News and Updates

The Marvel Disney Plus series follows Kamala Khan. As a young Pakistani-American teenager, Kamala is a quirky Avengers fangirl who idolizes the heroes who saved her world, especially Captain Marvel. While she prefers to live in her superhero fantasies and focus less on her real life, her dreams become a reality when she comes into superpowers of her own.

Wielding mysterious bracelets that allow her to augment her mass, Kamala gets to be the superhero she's always wanted to be. But she quickly discovers that being a hero is harder and more dangerous than it looks.

Ms. Marvel is expected to be a limited series with only six episodes.
