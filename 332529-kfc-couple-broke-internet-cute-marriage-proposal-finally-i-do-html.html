KFC couple that broke the internet with cute marriage proposal finally say I Do

- Hector and Nonhlanhla said I Do in the classy Theba Eco Hotel in South Africa

- The couple became a darling to many after Hector went down on one knee in a KFC restaurant to propose to his lover

- The bride aged 28 and the groom who is 37 became a national couple of the year 2019

A couple that broke the internet with a unique marriage proposal at KFC Evaton Mall, Vall, in South Africa has finally said I Do.

Nonhlanhla Soldaat aged 28 and Hector Mkansi, 37, professed their vows on Tuesday, December 31, 2019, in a colourful wedding ceremony graced by friends and relatives.
