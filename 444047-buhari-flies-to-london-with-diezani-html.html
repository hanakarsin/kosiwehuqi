Buhari Flies To London With Diezani

The president-elect, Muhammadu Buhari, jets on a British Airways to London with controversial Diezani Alison-Madueke, Minister of Petroleum Resources today, May 22.

Sahara Reporters says sources claimed that Buhari was on a planned trip.
